Rules

To play the game simply press start game and start choosing either rock, paper, scissors, spock or lizard.

The computer will automatically choose something and the result of the round will show below the buttons.

There will be 5 rounds and the winner will be announced at the end of the 5 rounds.

For the specifics of playing, please see below:
